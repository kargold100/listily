Tips for donating goods

✓ Food donations — check use-by dates, non-perishables preferred. Long-life milk, canned goods, pasta, rice and baby formula are always needed.
✓ Clothing — clean, wearable condition. Underwear and socks must be new. Winter coats, work clothing and school uniforms are high-demand items.
✓ Toys & books — clean and in working condition. Age labels removed. Picture books, puzzles and educational toys are particularly welcome.
✓ Furniture — contact the organisation first to confirm they can accept and store large items. GIVIT is a great platform for matching furniture donors with recipients.
