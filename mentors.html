Our mission

What Listily stands for

We believe every local business deserves to be found, every opportunity deserves to reach the right person, and every community member deserves access to guidance.

Trust through verification

Every business and opportunity on Listily is reviewed by our admin team before going live. No spam, no fakes — just genuine local listings.

Community first

We're built around Australian suburbs — not algorithms. From Point Cook to Cairns, we focus on the people and businesses that make communities thrive.

Mentorship matters

Our mentors section connects people with experienced local professionals who volunteer their knowledge — because the best guidance is often found close to home.

Support when it counts

We list emergency and crisis services alongside local businesses — because a community directory should serve the whole community, especially when it matters most.

The mentor programme

More than a directory

Listily's mentor network connects community members with experienced local professionals who volunteer their time to guide others.

  • Mentors register their speciality, experience and how they want to be contacted
  • They choose whether to publish their phone number or keep contact via email only
  • All mentors are admin-verified before appearing publicly
  • Covers trades, business, finance, arts, wellness, sport, education and more
  • 100% free — both for mentors and mentees

Common questions

FAQ

Is it free to list my business on Listily?
Yes — completely free. There are no listing fees, no subscription costs, and no hidden charges. Listily is a community-driven directory and we intend to keep basic listings free always.
How long does it take to get listed?
Our admin team reviews all submissions within 24 hours. Once approved, your listing goes live immediately and is searchable across all relevant category and suburb filters.
Can I update my listing after it's published?
At the moment, updates are handled by contacting us directly via the email below. Self-service editing is on our roadmap for Phase 4 — business owner accounts with a dashboard to manage your own listing.
How does the mentor programme work?
Mentors register via the "Become a mentor" form and choose their areas of speciality, how they prefer to connect (in-person, video call or phone), and whether to display their phone number publicly. All mentor profiles are reviewed before going live. Connecting with a mentor is free for both parties — Listily facilitates the connection, but the mentoring relationship itself is between you.
Why are emergency and support services listed?
We believe a community directory should serve the whole community. When someone searches for local services, they may need more than a plumber or a café — they might need crisis support, financial counselling or health advice. Listing verified national and state services alongside local businesses is part of our commitment to community wellbeing.
Is Listily available across all of Australia?
Yes. While our initial focus was on western Melbourne suburbs, Listily is built to cover all 8 states and territories. We currently have listings across VIC, NSW, QLD and WA, with SA, TAS, ACT and NT growing. Any Australian business, opportunity or mentor can register regardless of location.
How do I report an inaccurate or spam listing?
Email us at the address below with the listing name and what you believe is inaccurate or inappropriate. We review all reports within 24 hours and will update or remove listings that don't meet our standards.
